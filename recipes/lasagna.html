Lasagna

World's Best Lasagna

A recipe submitted to Allrecipes more than 20 years ago.
With more than 20 000 reviews and a strong 4,8 stars,
this is a timeless recipe that potrays when simple is the best.

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage
  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Water
  • Spices
  1. Brown the Italian sausage and ground beef with olive oil and butter
  2. Add crushed garlic, then add tomato paste and crushed tomatoes
  3. Let it cook then add water and spices to taste
